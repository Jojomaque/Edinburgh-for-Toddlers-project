My Go To Places

The National Museum of Scotland

The National Museum of Scotland is a great place to take your little ones. It's a huge museum with lots of interactive exhibits and activities for children of all ages. There are also plenty of cafes and restaurants in the museum, so you can take a break and refuel before heading back out to explore more.

The museum is free to enter, but donations are welcome. It's open every day from 10am to 5pm, so you can visit whenever suits you best.
